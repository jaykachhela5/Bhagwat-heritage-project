🌍 Our Seva – Social Service Initiatives

About Bhagwat Heritage

At Bhagwat Heritage Service Foundation Trust, Seva is the living expression of Bhagwat Dharma. Guided by the teachings of Bhagwan Shri Krishna, we are committed to serving humanity with compassion, humility, and devotion. Our mission is not only to preserve spiritual wisdom but also to transform lives through meaningful social service.


We believe that true devotion is shown through selfless service. Every initiative we undertake focuses on:

Supporting the needy and underprivileged

Promoting education and awareness

Protecting the environment

Encouraging global unity through compassion

Serving society without discrimination
