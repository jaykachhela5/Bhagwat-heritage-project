Break the Chains of Addiction

We help individuals recover from alcohol, drugs, tobacco, gambling and digital addiction through holistic rehabilitation programs.

Our Mission

To build an addiction free society by providing medical, psychological and spiritual rehabilitation support.

We focus on prevention, recovery and reintegration.

Types of Addiction We Address

Alcohol Addiction

Structured detox and therapy programs.

Drug Addiction

Medical supervision and recovery support.

Tobacco & Smoking

Nicotine de-addiction programs.

Gambling

Behavioral therapy and financial recovery planning.

Digital Addiction

Screen control and mental wellness programs.

Prescription Misuse

Safe medical tapering process.

Rehabilitation Process

1. Initial Consultation

Confidential assessment with counsellors.

2. Medical Evaluation

Health and psychological evaluation.

3. Detoxification

Supervised detox phase.

4. Therapy & Counseling

Individual and group therapy sessions.

5. Reintegration

Family counselling and job assistance.
