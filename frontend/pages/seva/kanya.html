Bhagwat Heritage Kanyadaan Seva ensures that every daughter receives marriage support with dignity, love and cultural values.

About Kanyadaan Seva

Kanyadaan Seva is a sacred initiative dedicated to supporting underprivileged daughters with financial and material assistance for marriage ceremonies.

We believe that every daughter deserves a respectful and joyous beginning to her married life. Through community support and transparent donation management, we provide essential help.

What We Provide

Marriage Financial Support

Direct financial aid for wedding expenses.

Essential Marriage Kit

Clothing, utensils and household essentials.

Ceremony Arrangement

Basic arrangements for dignified wedding ceremony.

Legal Documentation Help

Marriage registration assistance.
